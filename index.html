About Channel Dungeons

Channel Dungeons is an MMORPG played entirely in Discord, emphasizing exploration and collaboration. Delve into dungeons, battle fearsome monsters, discover valuable loot, and level up your hero!

Experience a shared, persistent world where you can encounter other players from various Discord servers while exploring dungeons solo or with friends.

How to Play

1

Create a Hero

Begin your adventure by creating a hero in your home town! Simply click the "Create a Hero" button or type /create_hero in Discord.

2

Explore Dungeons

Start exploring dungeons by using /move or pressing one of the "Go [direction]" buttons.

3

Find Loot & Level Up

Defeat monsters to discover valuable loot, gain experience, and level up your hero! If you ever get lost, use /hero to locate yourself.
