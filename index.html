About Channel Dungeons

Channel Dungeons is an MMORPG played entirely in Discord. The game focuses on exploration and collaboration. Players explore dungeons, fight monsters, get loot and level up.

Channel Dungeons uses a shared persistent world. This means that, while you explore dungeons alone or with your friends, you can run into other players - even from different servers.

How to Play

1

Create a Hero

To start playing create a new hero in your home town. Either use the "Create a Hero" button or type /create_hero.

2

Explore Dungeons

Start exploring dungeons by using /move or pressing one of the "Go [direction]" buttons.

3

Find Loot & Level Up

Defeat monsters to find valuable loot and gain experience to level up your hero. If you ever get lost use /hero.
