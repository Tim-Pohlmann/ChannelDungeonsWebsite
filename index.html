Welcome to Channel Dungeons!

Channel Dungeons is an MMORPG played entirely in Discord. The game focuses on exploration and collaboration. Players explore dungeons, fight monsters, get loot and level up.

Channel Dungeons uses a shared persistent world. This means that, while you explore dungeons alone or with your friends, you can run into other players - even from different servers.

Explore

Navigate through procedurally generated dungeons with unique layouts and challenges.

Battle

Fight monsters using a strategic combat system with various abilities and tactics.

Collect

Discover rare items, weapons, and armor to strengthen your hero.

Collaborate

Team up with friends or meet new allies in the shared persistent world.

How to Play

1

Create a Hero

To start playing, create a new hero in your home town. Either use the "Create a Hero" button or type /create_hero.

2

Explore Dungeons

Start exploring dungeons by using /move or pressing one of the "Go [direction]" buttons.

3

Battle & Loot

Encounter monsters, defeat them in battle, and collect valuable loot to strengthen your character.

4

Level Up

Gain experience points and level up your hero to unlock new abilities and increase your stats.

Helpful Tips:

  • When you die, you can respawn in your home town with /respawn.
  • If you ever get lost, use /hero to see your current status and location.
  • Team up with friends to tackle more challenging dungeons and bosses.

Game Features

Shared Persistent World

Explore a living world where your actions have consequences and you can encounter other players from different Discord servers.

Procedural Dungeons

Every dungeon is unique with procedurally generated layouts, ensuring a fresh experience every time you play.

Strategic Combat

Engage in tactical battles with a variety of abilities, items, and strategies to overcome challenging foes.

Rich Progression

Level up your hero, discover rare equipment, and customize your character to suit your playstyle.
